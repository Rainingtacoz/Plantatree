About Us

PlantATree is a Webapp that anyone can use to buy trees online from a national chain of 10 nurseries I own. The company is called TreeCo. The company imports, grows and sells trees as well as gardening products related to growing and maintaining trees. Our main buyers are:

Landscape gardeners – doing work for others. My main market is these people. The tool should support landscape gardeners.

Housing Developers – for new subdivisions public spaces. High volume, specific tree types. Very profitable but sporadic. App not aimed at this group specifically.

Local Councils – for public places. Medium volume. Very profitable and return business, but sporadic. App not aimed at this group so much.
