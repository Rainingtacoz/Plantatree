TreeBay

TreeCo is a company where you can buy trees at different stages for any projects from small to large. TreeCo has made a Web app that allows customers to buy from anywhere in New Zealand using the power of smartphones.

TreeCo also encourages that loyal customers are respected and looked after with special deals that they can claim in checkout.

TreeCo allows the purchase of gardening tools and a knowledge base of information that will help the buyer keep their plants alive.
